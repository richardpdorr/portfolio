About Me

My name is Richard Dorr, I am a senior developer and website lead at uBreakiFix. I am considered a full stack developer with particular concentrations in VueJS (and also React) and CSS/SASS/LESS. I have been developing frontend and backend features using PHP and Javascript for numerous companies over the past 8 years. My favorite part of development is UI/UX and creating the best path that I can lead any of my users. I strongly believe the key features for any website or app are "simplicity", "realiability", and "ease of use".
